Oxford University Salsa Society is proud to present: The Oxford Salsa Ball 2018

Featuring special guest artists, workshops in all levels (beginners too!) and styles, a live salsa band, and stunning performances – including our very own Salsa Team debut show! The event will be spread across two rooms – one dedicated to the most classic Latin rythms of Salsa, Mambo and Chachacha, the other to Bachata, Kizomba and all things Sensual.

7.45pm: Doors open
8.00pm–9.00pm: Workshops in Salsa and Bachata
9.00pm-1.30am: Party to the best Latin rythms
10.00pm: Live Music Set
11.30pm: Live Shows
1.30am –- late: After-Party in a secret location!

VENUE:
Oxford Town Hall
St Aldate's, Oxford OX1 1BX
5 minutes walk from Gloucester Green Bus Station
10 minutes walk from Oxford Railway Station
